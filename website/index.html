Your music. Your rules.

Sonic Vault is a free, offline music player that puts you in control. Play from your local files, set your own cover art, sync lyrics, and shape your sound — no subscriptions, no streaming, no noise.

Download View on GitHub
Everything you need

Built for music lovers

No streaming, no accounts, no distractions. Just your local library, organized the way you want it.

🎵

Play from local files

Reads ID3 tags, embedded cover art, and audio properties directly from your music files. No import needed.

🖼️

Custom covers

Pick any image as album art for any song or playlist. Your library, your look.

📋

Playlists

Create, rename, reorder, and export playlists in M3U format. Full drag-and-drop support.

🎤

Synchronized lyrics

Load LRC files for karaoke-style lyrics that scroll in time with your music.

🎛️

10-band equalizer

Built-in equalizer with presets. Fine-tune your sound to match your headphones or room.

🔊

Volume normalization

Per-song volume leveling ensures consistent playback across your entire library.

Get started

Download Sonic Vault

Grab the latest release from GitHub. Choose your platform and you're set.

Windows

Standalone .exe, no installer needed

  1. Download SonicVault-windows.zip
  2. Extract the archive
  3. Run sonicvault.exe
Download for Windows

Android

APK for sideload installation

  1. Download app-release.apk
  2. Enable Install from unknown sources
  3. Open the APK to install
